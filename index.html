hello, it's me. i've thought about us for a long, long time... (what?)


quinten laurènce sol (they/them) is a creative peculiarity currently habited in rotterdam, the netherlands...
they write, sing, produce, mix, code and design, and also likes to discover new music, read, or just drift away from the world from time to time.

their current occupation is a solo musical project called INDIGO CHAMBER, which could be described as dreamy pop music with swoony falsettos, languid melodies, sultry synths, intimate poetry and ethereal guitarlines, originating from some place far away from our known universe.

currently a student of Music and Technology at HKU University of the Arts, they recently conquered their place in cyberspace because apparently, coding your own site is all the rage nowadays!

they are also in a band called HORIZONS where they make silky smooth sailin' yachtrock and easy-listening cocktail party AOR to escape from all your earthly troubles.



if you want to check out what they have made so far, you can navigate to the 'work' tab on the left.

they are also available for mixing, producing and recording duties for other people in a wide range of styles.
so if you are interested in their mixing work, please go to the 'contact' tab to shoot them a mail, and who knows something magical will happen!
